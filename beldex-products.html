🤖 Federated Learning

AI That Gets Smarter — Without Reading Your Messages

The AI improves over time using lessons from many phones. But your actual messages never leave your device.

01
📲
AI comes pre-installed on your phone
When you install BChat, a small AI is already there. It can spot harmful or abusive messages — even without an internet connection.
02
🔍
It reads messages on your device only
Every message is checked right there on your phone. Nothing is sent out. If something looks harmful, the AI can warn you, blur, or block it.
03
📊
Only "what it learned" is shared — not the message
If the AI made a mistake, your phone learns from it. It then sends only tiny math signals to help improve the model — never the actual message content.
04
🔄
Updates from many phones are combined
These signals from thousands of phones are merged into one improved AI. No personal data is ever collected or stored anywhere.
05
⬇️
The smarter AI comes back to your phone
The improved model is sent to all devices. Only the AI itself moves — not your messages, contacts, or any personal data.
