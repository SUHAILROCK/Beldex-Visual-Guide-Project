CryptoNote is a blockchain design that protects your transaction details.

One-Time Addresses

Hide the Receiver

Every time someone sends BDX, a unique one-time address is created on the blockchain.

‚úî Payments cannot be publicly linked
‚úî Your wallet balance is not visible to others

Even if you receive 100 payments, they cannot be connected together.

Ring Signatures

Hide the Sender

When you send BDX, your payment is mixed with a small group of other payments.

So on the blockchain it looks like:

"One of these people sent it."

But nobody can tell which one.
This keeps the sender private.

Why Beldex Uses CryptoNote

Beldex chose CryptoNote because:

  • Privacy is automatic
  • No "transparent mode"
  • Coins cannot be tracked easily
  • It improves digital cash behavior
  • It has been tested for many years

CryptoNote gives Beldex its privacy foundation.
Beldex builds the larger ecosystem on top of it.
