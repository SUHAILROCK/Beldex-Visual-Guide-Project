What is EVM Integration?

The Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) is the standard smart contract execution environment used by Ethereum and most major blockchains. Integrating EVM compatibility into Beldex enables developers to deploy Solidity smart contracts directly on Beldex.

This means the entire Ethereum developer toolkit — MetaMask, Hardhat, OpenZeppelin — works natively with Beldex, while transactions benefit from Beldex's privacy layer.

Key Achievement: Beldex becomes one of the few privacy-focused blockchains with full EVM compatibility, bridging the gap between privacy and programmability.

How It Works

Beldex's EVM layer operates alongside the native CryptoNote/privacy layer:

Solidity Contracts

Write and deploy smart contracts in Solidity exactly as you would on Ethereum. Full ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1155 support.

Web3 Compatible

MetaMask, Ethers.js, Web3.js, Hardhat — all standard Ethereum tools connect to Beldex's EVM endpoint.

Privacy Bridge

Assets can move between the private UTXO layer and the EVM layer, enabling private accumulation with programmable utility.

Low Fees

Beldex's PoS consensus provides significantly lower gas fees compared to Ethereum mainnet.
