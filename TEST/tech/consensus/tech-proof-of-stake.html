What is Proof of Stake?

Proof of Stake (PoS) is a consensus mechanism where validators are chosen to create new blocks based on the amount of cryptocurrency they "stake" (lock up) as collateral, rather than competing through computational power like in Proof of Work.

Beldex transitioned from Proof of Work to Proof of Stake in December 2021 through a successful hardfork. This made the network dramatically more energy-efficient, faster, and accessible â€” while maintaining the same strong privacy guarantees through RingCT and stealth addresses.

Key Principle: Instead of spending electricity to mine blocks, validators lock their BDX as a security deposit. Honest validators earn rewards; dishonest ones risk losing their stake.

Why Beldex Migrated to PoS

  • Environmental responsibility: PoW mining consumed enormous energy. PoS reduces consumption by over 99%, making Beldex environmentally sustainable.
  • Lower barrier to entry: Running a validator no longer requires expensive mining hardware, allowing more participants to contribute to network security.
  • Masternode synergy: PoS works naturally with Beldex's masternode infrastructure, creating a unified incentive structure for block production and ecosystem services.
  • Privacy preserved: The migration to PoS did not compromise any privacy features â€” RingCT, stealth addresses, and ring signatures continue to work identically.
