🫆 Device Fingerprinting

There is a Way to Recognise You That Has Nothing to Do With Cookies

Even if you clear every cookie, websites can still recognise you. They read tiny details about your device. Every device is unique enough to identify you. Beldex answers every test with made-up data.

🖼️
The Screen Drawing Test
A site draws an invisible shape. Your screen renders it slightly differently than every other device. The result is unique to your device.
🛡 Beldex returns fake results. Every user looks identical. The test fails.
🔊
The Silent Sound Test
A site plays a sound you cannot hear. Your audio hardware processes it in a unique way. It produces a tiny number like 0.0001234 that is specific to your device.
🛡 Beldex gives every site the same standard number. Your hardware is never revealed.
🔤
The Font & Screen Size Test
Every device has a different set of fonts and screen size. A site can ask for both. That combination identifies you even if your IP address changes.
🛡 Beldex reports only basic fonts and a rounded screen size. Nothing specific is shared.
🔗
Tracking Across Many Sites
One ad company embeds on thousands of sites. Your fingerprint is the same everywhere. They link all your visits together without any cookies.
🛡 Beldex sends the same fake fingerprint to every site. Nothing can be linked to you.
